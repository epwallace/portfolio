about me

I'm a Web Developer based in Toronto with a background in Molecular Genetics and Computer Science. I'm passionate about learning: I love researching, exploring new concepts, and sharing my findings with others. I value clarity and simplicity, and I bring that approach into my development. Clean, elegant, intuitive code brings a smile to my face, and I aim to delight others with my programming. When I'm not coding, you can find me tending to my collection of epiphytic plants, playing with cats, or watching spooky movies.
